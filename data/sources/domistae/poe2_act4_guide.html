Routing Notes

  • ◆ Weapon Fragments and Matiki are randomized across the four islands each league — Kingsmarch dialogue tells you which
  • ◆ Magma Twins (Volcanic Warrens) — kill order matters: Lightning first → Ruby Ring · Fire first → Topaz Ring
  • ◆ Mimok the Enslaved (Isle of Kin) drops a guaranteed Tier 4 Support Gem
  • ◆ Halls of the Dead tattoo: pick +5% Fire/Cold/Lightning Resistance for most builds (Str/Dex/Int alternative for stat-thresholds)
  • ◆ Eye of Hinekora — hidden chest behind waterfall = Skill Gem Lv12 + Spirit Gem Lv12
  • ◆ Blind Beast (Isle of Kin) drops Greater Blank Rune in 0.4 (skill points moved to Omniphobia)
  • ◆ 0.5: Greater Orbs of Transmutation / Augmentation can now begin dropping in Act 4 (min mod level dropped 55 → 44)
  • ◆ 0.5: monster density reduced in the back half of the campaign — Act 4 is comparatively XP-rich, but pace still tightens overall

⚡ Speedrun Tips (Community)

  • ◆ Pre-organize your stash on Standard a few days before league start — tab names, layouts and highlights carry over to your fresh league character. Saves a chunk of first-town time.
  • ◆ Save artificers / runes / whetstones for your weapon at the end of Act 4 once you know what you're carrying into Interludes/Maps.
  • ◆ Aim for ~5 hours campaign without layout study — pure urgency + magic-pack focus + breakpoint-aware crafting gets you there.
