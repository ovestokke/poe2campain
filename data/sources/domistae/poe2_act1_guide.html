Routing Notes

  • ◆ Cemetery: do Draven first, then Asinia (drops scale with zone level)
  • ◆ Hunting Grounds league mech = Exalted Orb
  • ◆ Cemetery league mech = Regal Orb (save for lvl 16 or 28 base)
  • ◆ Aim to be Lvl 14 entering Geonor fight

⚡ Speedrun Tips (Community)

  • ◆ Play with urgency. Minimize town time and never stop moving in a zone — even inefficient progress beats no progress.
  • ◆ Focus magic packs. Blue-name (magic) monsters give ~3.5× the XP of equivalent whites. Skip white packs unless you're under-leveled.
  • ◆ Don't mule. Push to the Red Vale weapon racks — they're guaranteed to drop a 2nd-tier Martial weapon (long quarterstaff, smithing hammer, tense crossbow).
  • ◆ Ration regals/exalts. 1–2 regals per act, ideally on weapon bases that hit modifier breakpoints. Save exalts.
  • ◆ Stay on the XP curve. Margin of safety = 3 levels under area + 1 per 16 character levels. Push XP when you fall outside it.
